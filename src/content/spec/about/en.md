# Hello👋, this is cmchen's collection of life moments

Sometimes, I suddenly pause

Like at dusk, after connecting the last wire, I look up and see that maple tree outside the window, its leaves having turned completely red without me noticing. Or scrolling through my phone album, stumbling upon a random snapshot of the sky, unable to recall why I took it that day, yet remembering how light the wind was

These moments are fragmented, tiny, nothing extraordinary. But they are like light, leaking through the cracks of day-to-day life

I've thought for a long time, why start this blog?

No scenery worth showing off, no profound philosophies to share

Just wanted to find a place to gather this light from the cracks

Those roads I walked alone, those winds I shared with friends, those nights I dragged my exhausted body back to the dorm, those dusks when I ran laps on the playground while troubled by difficult problems, those sudden memories of old things that have already grown a bit blurry

They are my memory fragments. Pieced together, they are probably who I am now

So this place will be quiet. Updates might be slow, writings might lack structure. But every post is real, grown out of life

If you happen to pass by and find some fragment familiar, that would be wonderful

My name is cmchen, still learning to coexist with problems, and learning to carve out cracks in the cyclical routine

Welcome to my cracks
