# Hello 👋, This is where cmchen collects some moments of life

Sometimes, I suddenly pause

Like when I finish connecting the last wire at dusk, look up and see the maple tree outside the window, its leaves having turned crimson without me noticing. Or when scrolling through my phone album, stumbling upon a casual shot of the sky, unable to recall why I took it that day, but remembering how gentle the breeze was.

These moments, fragmented and tiny, nothing extraordinary. Yet they slip through the cracks of my daily routine, like rays of light.

I've thought for a long time: why start this blog?

No breathtaking scenery to show off, no profound philosophies to share.

I just want to find a place to gather these slivers of light from the cracks.

The paths I've walked alone, the breezes shared with friends, the nights I dragged my weary body back to the dorm, the dusks when I ran laps on the track while tangled in tough problems, those suddenly-remembered old memories that have grown a bit hazy.

They are fragments of my memory. Pieced together, they roughly form who I am now.

So this place will be quiet. Updates might be slow, writings might lack structure. But every post is genuine, grown from life itself.

If you happen to pass by and find a fragment that feels familiar, that's wonderful.

I'm cmchen, still learning to coexist with problems, and learning to carve out cracks in the cyclical routine of daily life.

Welcome to my cracks.
